Designer & Developer.

Hello world. I’m Eric Gardner, a designer and developer living in Los Angeles.

Currently I’m imagining the future of art publishing at the Getty as digital publication developer. Previously I worked at the Hammer Museum. I spend about equal parts of my time on visual design and writing code (mainly Ruby and Javascript).

My other interests include analog and digital photography, bicycling, and social justice.

Feel free to view my past work, or to read some of my writing. This site was hand-built using Middleman, a CMS-free static site generator, and is still under active development.
