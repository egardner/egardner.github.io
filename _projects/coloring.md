My client wanted a fun interactive welcome gift for new students to their school. I created a suite of custom illustrations, inspired by the favorite areas of the school and had this little pocket-sized coloring book produced by a local printer. We used an opaque white ink on a craft paper cover. 

My role: art direction, custom illustration, book design, print production
